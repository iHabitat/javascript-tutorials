梦想

“当我年轻的时候，我梦想改变这个世界；当我成熟以后，我发现我不能够改变这个世界，我将目光缩短了些，决定只改变我的国家；当我进入暮年以后，我发现我不能够改变我们的国家，我的最后愿望仅仅是改变一下我的家庭，但是，这也不可能。当我现在躺在床上，行将就木时，我突然意识到：如果一开始我仅仅去改变我自己，然后，我可能改变我的家庭；在家人的帮助和鼓励下，我可能为国家做一些事情；然后，谁知道呢?我甚至可能改变这个世界。”

"When I was young, I dreamed of changing the world; When I was mature, I found that I could not change the world. I made my eyes shorter and decided to change my country only. When I was old, I found that I could not change our country, and my last wish was to change my family, but that was impossible. When I was lying in bed and dying, I suddenly realized that if I had only changed myself at first, then I might have changed my family. With the help and encouragement of my family, I might do something for the country; And then, who knows? I might even change the world."
